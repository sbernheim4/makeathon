The Makeathon is an event in which students form teams to compete against one another. A team works to design develop and implement their own ideas from start to finish and each team works on a project which is based off of a theme given by Brandeis. Teams have 24 hours to build their ideas into presentable working models which will then be judged by members of the Brandeis Community and people from major companies including Onshape, AutoDesk and more.
